How it works

Three steps. That's it.

01

Swipe local events

Tinder-style discovery, tuned to your interests and neighborhood. Right for interested. Up for going. Left to skip. See what's happening around you in seconds.

02

Match with your people

When a friend — or a friend of a friend — is into the same event, pakt tells you instantly. That's the magic moment. Now you have a plan.

03

Show up together

Group chats form automatically when 3+ friends are going. Coordinate in one place. Make it happen. Look back on nights worth remembering.

The swipe feed

Events curated by your crew.

Most apps show you content designed to keep you scrolling. pakt shows you events that get you outside. Events your friends are interested in float to the top — because the best plans start with someone you already love.
