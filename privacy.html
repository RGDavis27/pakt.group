Privacy Policy

Last updated · January 1, 2026

pakt, Inc. ("pakt," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the Service.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information (name, email address, phone number, date of birth)
  • Profile information (photo, bio, interests, hometown)
  • Social connections you choose to import or add manually
  • Content you post, share, or send through the Service
  • Communications with our support team

Information collected automatically:

  • Device identifiers and operating system information
  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps)
  • Usage data (events you swipe on, matches made, RSVPs, attendance)
  • City-level location for event discovery (precise GPS is never tracked in the background)
  • App performance and crash reports

Information from third parties:

  • Spotify: Artist preferences and listening history (only with your explicit authorization)
  • Eventbrite / Ticketmaster: Event data and ticketing status
  • Social platforms: Basic profile information if you sign in via a third-party provider

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the Service, including powering our Social Heat algorithm
  • Match you with friends who have expressed interest in the same events
  • Personalize event recommendations based on your social graph and taste profile
  • Facilitate group chat creation and communication features
  • Send you push notifications and in-app messages (you can opt out at any time)
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Period.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With other pakt users: Your profile and RSVP status are visible to your confirmed friends. Friends-of-friends visibility is opt-in only.
  • With service providers: Trusted vendors (cloud hosting, analytics, push notifications) who are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • For legal reasons: If required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of pakt, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your information may transfer to the successor entity.

4. Friend-of-Friend Visibility

By default, your RSVP status is only visible to confirmed friends. The friends-of-friends (FoF) feature is opt-in only and can be toggled at any time in your privacy settings.

5. Location Data

We use your city-level location to surface nearby events. Precise GPS tracking is not required and is never performed in the background. You can revoke location permission at any time through your device settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Group chats are automatically archived 7 days after the associated event ends. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing privacy@pakt.app.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data; opt out of marketing communications; withdraw consent for optional data processing; and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (EU/UK residents). Contact us at privacy@pakt.app.

8. Children's Privacy

pakt is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security including encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest, JWT authentication with token rotation, E2E encrypted messages, and regular security audits.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification. Your continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

  • Mail: pakt, Inc. · Brooklyn, NY
